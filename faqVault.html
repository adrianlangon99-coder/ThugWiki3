Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about navigating the lore database and participating in the community.

ThugWiki is the #1 community-driven encyclopedia dedicated to documenting the icons, viral audios, and memes that have shaped internet culture. We act as a centralized archive to preserve history and prevent the spread of false lore.

To maintain the highest quality of accuracy and prevent trolling or "false lore," public editing is currently disabled. If you notice an error or have new information, you can submit a request through the Report page for our curators to review.

You can access hundreds of classic, retired, and iconic videos by clicking the "Vault Access" banner located on the main app page. Please note that an account is required to view certain archived materials.

We are always looking to expand the database! You can suggest a new creator by navigating to the Support page. Be sure to include their main social handles, a brief overview of their lore, and links to their most viral audios.

We take our community guidelines very seriously. Spamming the discussion boards, harassing users, or intentionally spreading false lore will result in an immediate, permanent ban from the ThugWiki network.
